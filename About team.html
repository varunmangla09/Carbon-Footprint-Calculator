About Our Project

  • Purpose: The site helps users track, calculate, and reduce their carbon footprint through detailed data input and personalized recommendations.
  • Features:
    • Carbon Footprint Calculator: Allows users to input data on fuel consumption, energy use, transportation habits, waste production, and business travel to calculate their total carbon footprint.
    • Historical Data Storage: Users can view and manage past carbon footprint records, with options to clear or update data.
    • Personalized Recommendations: Provides tailored tips and suggestions for reducing emissions based on the userâ€™s specific activities and inputs.
    • Educational Resources: Links to articles, guides, and resources on climate action, sustainability practices, and carbon footprint reduction.
    • User Interface: Designed for ease of use with a fixed navigation menu and a responsive design for accessibility across devices.
    • Data Handling: Utilizes emission factors to compute accurate carbon footprints and ensures user data is stored securely.
    • Target Audience: Individuals and businesses interested in monitoring and minimizing their carbon footprint and enhancing their sustainability practices.
